The Reality

Everyone is talking about AI. Very few are ready for it.

The technology has arrived. Large language models, autonomous agents, generative tools. They're powerful, accessible, and getting better every quarter. That's not the hard part anymore.

The hard part is everything underneath. Your data is fragmented across dozens of systems. Your teams don't have a shared view of what's true. Your processes were built for a world that didn't have these tools.

Bolting AI onto that foundation doesn't create value. It creates expensive confusion.

The businesses getting real ROI from AI right now aren't the ones moving fastest. They're the ones who understood what needed to be true about their data, their operations, and their people before they built anything.
